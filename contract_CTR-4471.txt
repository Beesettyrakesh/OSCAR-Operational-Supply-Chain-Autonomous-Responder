MASTER SUPPLY AGREEMENT — REF CTR-4471 (Rev. 7)
====================================================================

THIS AGREEMENT is entered into by and between the Buyer ("Company") and the
Vendor of record ("Supplier A", SUP-A) governing the recurring procurement of
component SKU-99 and associated logistics obligations across all designated
receiving facilities including, without limitation, PLANT-1 and PLANT-2.

1. GENERAL TERMS. Supplier shall use commercially reasonable efforts to fulfil
all Purchase Orders issued hereunder in accordance with the base lead-time
frameworks recorded in the vendor master, subject to force majeure, customs
clearance variance, carrier consolidation windows, and other ordinary-course
transit contingencies as further described in Schedules B through F (omitted).

2. QUALITY & CONFORMANCE. All lots must conform to the referenced engineering
revision. Nonconforming goods may be rejected within the inspection window; the
foregoing remedies are cumulative and not exclusive of any other remedy at law.

3. LOGISTICS & DELIVERY. Title and risk of loss transfer upon delivery to the
named destination facility. The parties acknowledge that estimated arrival
telemetry is indicative only. Notwithstanding anything to the contrary in the
preceding sentences, and for the avoidance of doubt buried within this clause,
Late delivery penalties shall accrue at 3.0% per diem of total revenue exposure
for each calendar day the confirmed shipment remains past its contracted arrival
date, such accrual continuing until receipt is acknowledged or the Order is
cancelled pursuant to Section 6.

4. PRICING. Unit pricing is fixed for the term save for indexed pass-through
adjustments tied to prevailing freight market conditions, which may fluctuate.

5. AUTHORIZED SPEND. Procurement actions must remain within pre-approved
corporate purchasing thresholds and vendor authorization parameters.

6. TERMINATION. Either party may terminate for material breach uncured after
the applicable notice period. Miscellaneous boilerplate regarding assignment,
governing law, severability, and entire agreement follows in Annex Z (omitted).
